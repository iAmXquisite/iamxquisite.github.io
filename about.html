About Us

Virtual reality has been around for a long time. It is a topic that is pretty hot recently with introductions of many forms of it. There are many that use a screen in front of the user with glasses type wear to see the digital world in front of them and the other way uses the world around you. The Hololens is the one that we liked out of all of them. It is the first untethered and best looking one out there. We all liked the idea and wanted to create a website that were to sell software that would use the Hololens to give the user as much freedom as they want in their own comfort, rather than being tethered and tied to an expensive computer, they could use it freely for any activity they would like.


Each one of us wanted to work on different things. Jiovanni wanted to work on the structure and the HTML/(Javascript?) for the website. He made the template, editable regions and the number of page we wanted to work with. Cesar brought up the Idea on what to work with, looked up information about it, filled editable regions with context and went over ideas with the group. Chris worked on the design of the site, the color, and the actual logo for the site, arraigning the site and the look/feel of it using Photoshop for custom logos and images we wanted to mix.


We all worked on every page together after the template was done. The format and the look of it, the feel once Chris brought the look and design for it. Jiovanni worked on any issues that came up with collapsible regions, forms and buttons. Cesar did the grammar and spell checking, made sure the editable regions had context and filled them with any detail that needed to be done.


Images we got for the icons in our pricing/experience tab were found from a free vector site, so we could scale the images without losing the quality. The Hololens home video was found in the Microsoft Hololens site. We found it in the site and inspected the element to get the code for the video, put it on to our site and looped it.
